Spyglass POC

Spyglass is a 3-week, augmented reality (AR) proof of concept (POC) by Valtech and Contentstack.

The Spyglass POC uses AR to help demystify skincare products in a retail and home setting. We explain much more in the behind-the-scenes blogposts.

This prototype is very much a proof of concept. You may find some things still do not work as you might as expect. Right now, the demo has three scenarios, but they are not seamlessly connected to each other.

Visit our Spyglass demo page for more information: https://spyglass.valtech.engineering/
